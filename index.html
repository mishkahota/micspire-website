About MICSPIRE

\

Micspire is a public speaking program designed to help middle school students build confidence, communication skills, and the ability to express their ideas clearly. It focuses on teaching practical speaking skills through interactive lessons, including storytelling, persuasive speaking, and impromptu practice.

\

The program is designed to be engaging, supportive, and accessible, especially for students who may feel nervous about speaking in front of others.

\

Micspire is for middle school students who want to become more confident speakers, whether they are just starting to overcome stage fright or already looking to refine their communication skills. It is especially helpful for students who want to improve class presentations, participate more actively in school, or develop leadership and expression skills early on.

What We Offer

\
\
\

Persuasive Writing

\

Students begin their Micspire journey by learning how to structure clear and compelling arguments through persuasive writing. They focus on organizing ideas, using strong reasoning, and building confidence in expressing their opinions effectively.

\
\
\

Poetry Expression

\

Students explore creative expression through poetry, focusing on rhythm, emotion, and voice. They learn how to use language intentionally to express thoughts and feelings in a powerful and artistic way.

\
\
\

Narrative Storytelling

\

Students learn how to craft engaging stories with strong structure, vivid detail, and emotional impact. This session helps them bring personal or fictional experiences to life in a way that captures and holds an audience's attention.

About the Founder

\

Mishka Hota

\

Micspire started from a simple observation: most students are expected to speak confidently in front of others, but very few are actually taught how. I wanted to create a space where middle school students could learn those skills early\'97without pressure, judgment, or fear of being "wrong." What began as an idea quickly became a program focused on helping students find their voice through structured, supportive speaking practice.

\

As the founder, my goal is to make communication feel accessible and natural, not intimidating. I believe confidence in speaking is not something you\'92re born with\'97it\'92s something you build through practice, feedback, and the right environment. Micspire exists to give students that environment, and to help them grow into speakers who can express their ideas clearly, creatively, and with purpose.
